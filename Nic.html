Our Values: The Foundation

From the start of walking down the road of building something, we wanted to make sure whatever we were a part of stood on values and ACTUALLY lived and worked those values. No one is perfect, and I know I'll personally mess up at times, but I always want to filter the decisions being made through this set of values and ensure they set the tone for everything we do.

Relationships – People Over Profit, Always

Business isn't a transaction, it's personal. We build genuine partnerships that last, valuing long-term connection over short-term gains. We treat people right because it matters to us - not because it's "good business." Trust, respect, and fairness aren't optional. They're how we operate.

Stewardship – Build with Integrity, Execute with Honor

We share information openly and follow through on commitments. When we make mistakes, we own them and extract the lessons. We protect both our team and our partners because their success is our success. Trust grows from consistent actions, not promises.

Improvement – Consistent Growth Over Time

Nothing in nature stays still, it either grows or dies. We need continuous evolution to feel alive and purposeful. When we stop learning, we stagnate. When we embrace change and push ourselves daily, we thrive. We choose growth because it energizes us, challenges us, and ensures we're building something that lasts.

Wisdom – Honor Experience, Welcome Change

We balance experienced perspectives with fresh thinking, data with discernment, and action with reflection. This blend of stability and innovation creates space for meaningful insights. We stay humble enough to learn, thoughtful enough to question, and confident enough to act.

Courage – Take the Hard Road, Do What Others Won't

We take on the hard problems others shy away from. Meaningful challenges energize us, they fulfill our need to contribute and grow. When the path gets difficult, our values keep us moving forward. Conviction drives us, not fear.

Revelry – Life Should Be Fun

Work should energize us, not drain us. When we have fun, we're more creative, more connected, and more effective. Positivity fuels momentum. If success doesn't feel good, we're doing it wrong. We choose joy because it brings out our best work.

Collaboration – The Smartest People in the Room Aren't Always in the Room

We don't believe in lone wolves. We move fast because we leverage collective intelligence, not individual ego. Diverse perspectives make us better. Different insights, backgrounds, and expertise create stronger solutions. The best execution happens together.

NineOneSix Systems: The Utility Layer

To quote Stephen: "We want NineOneSix to be like the power company, or Verizon—no one thinks about the power company, they just know they all have to use it." (I'm paraphrasing.)

The way we think about NineOneSix has morphed and changed significantly over the last two years, so I want to align and make sure we are on the same page with the most up-to-date and clear picture.

Vision

We envision a world where business infrastructure is as invisible and reliable as electricity - where every founder can build their dreams without sacrificing the relationships and moments that matter most. NineOneSix Systems will transform business ownership from a prison of endless tasks into a vehicle for passion, creativity, and human connection.

Mission:

At NineOneSix Systems, our mission is to build a decentralized core utility layer, intentionally diluted across dozens of aligned owners to prevent concentrated power, safeguard against corruption, and foster resilience and innovation through broad, equitable equity distribution. From this foundation, we will spin off multiple sub-companies, some directly affiliated, others independent, each aligned with our vision, featuring concentrated ownership for dedicated operators and stakeholders, with non-universal overlap to promote expertise, accountability, and majority stakes, as we collectively reshape the future of business.

What Makes Up the Utility Layer

At NineOneSix Systems, the foundational elements of our decentralized utility layer are embodied in our core sub-companies, each designed to provide seamless, reliable business infrastructure that empowers founders to focus on innovation and human connection.

Forge: Delivers cutting-edge software development and technology infrastructure, building the digital backbone that scales effortlessly like electricity.

Hearth: Handles business architecture, formation, and strategy, crafting robust frameworks to launch and guide ventures with clarity and purpose.

Anvil: Provides comprehensive business operations and HR services done for you, automating the essentials to free up time for what truly matters.

Crucible: Establishes peer networks and a rigorous quality assurance framework, fostering collaboration, excellence, and a self-reinforcing referral network.

Foundry: Serves as our sandbox, incubator, and accelerator, offering ready-to-deploy business frameworks for aspiring entrepreneurs. (We have the ideas and infrastructure in place, we just need someone to run them: EcoVolt, Housie, wanDRNest, Lucepta, Sustainable Farming, etc.)

Meridian 44: The Paradigm Shift We're Building

The Problem We're Attacking

We see two massive value extractions destroying professional expertise:

The AI Training Heist: OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google built $100B+ scraping expert content without compensation. The result? Mediocre AI creating medical errors, legal sanctions, and what Apple Research calls an "illusion of thinking." We're watching decades of professional knowledge get stolen, commoditized, and degraded.

The PE Strip-Mining Operation: 4,000+ physician practices acquired, 40% of dental consolidated, accounting and law firms disappearing daily. These professionals thought they were getting liquidity instead they became employees watching their life's work generate someone else's returns.

What We're Creating: The Expert-Owned Alternative

Meridian 44 is our answer - a complete inversion of the extraction model.

We're building infrastructure where:

  • Expert contributions become equity stakes through SAFE agreements
  • Knowledge generates ongoing royalties instead of one-time sales
  • Professionals keep their businesses while building proprietary tech assets
  • Custom solutions enhance expertise rather than replacing it with generic software

Our architecture: 44 specialized verticals, 4,000-6,000 verified experts, each documenting and contributing expertise to train AI that they actually own.

The Financial Engineering We've Developed

For our expert partners, we enable:

  • 35-45% taxable income reduction through strategic expense allocation
  • Ordinary income → capital gains conversion (17-27% rate differential)
  • $10M+ platform value creation from $500K-$1M investment
  • $1.5M+ licensing revenue generation over 10 years

For us, we're building:

  • Defensible moats through verified expert training data
  • Network effects through peer review and quality assurance
  • Recurring revenue through platform royalties and licensing
  • Exit optionality through distributed ownership that prevents hostile takeover

Why This Aligns With Everything We're Doing

Meridian 44 embodies our core thesis:

  • Decentralized ownership prevents the concentrated power we're fighting against
  • Utility layer thinking makes expert knowledge as accessible as electricity
  • Stewardship values ensure experts benefit from their contributions forever
  • Collaborative model proves the smartest people really aren't always in our room
